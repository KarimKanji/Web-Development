Time under tension

Time under tension means that in order to efficiently break down muscle tissue you need to have sufficient amount of stress on the specified muscle/muscles. This can be applied in practice in many ways. A general guideline is that every set you do should last about 40 sec. But thats not everything Another basic principle in weight lifting is eccentric and concentric movements. You need to control the weight during the entire range of motion and make sure that your eccentric movement is slower than your concentric movement.

Tearing down muscle

Tearing down muscle is how more muscle is built.
In order to build muscle you need stimulus
When you break down the muscle by training hard
the body repairs the broken muscle and it becomes
stronger. In order for this to happend you need:
Sufficient stimulus, protein, energy and water.

Mind-Muscle connection

The mind and muscle connection means that the person performing the excerise has good control over the muscle or muscles that is/are currently working. By really feeling the muscle contract, and by controlling the weight you improve your mind and muscle connection. By having a good mind and muscle connection you can better your progress in building muscle since the muscle you want to be working on is more isolated.

Nutrition

To build muscle you must have a good diet for many reasons. With a good and well rounded diet you make sure to have all the micro and macro nutrients you need to feel and function well. All macros are important but how much of each you consume each day is not as important as just simply getting enough protein and having energy. What you should "count" are your calories. Bodyfat comes down to calories in and calories out. If your calorie expendature is bigger than your intake you will burn fat. When it comes to protein you should aim to consume 1-1,5g of protein per lean mass kg. Protein also has a higher thermodynamic effect meaning that it takes more calories for the body to break down protein than it does for other macros. Protein also keeps you more satiated which means you will keep your cravings and hunger in controll.

Rest

Muscle is broken in the gym but it is built during rest. If you want to maximize your gains you have to sleep enough. Rest can infact be one of the biggest restricting factors when it comes to building muslce. Lacking in this department will for sure affect your gains, mood and energy.

Protocol

Depending on what your goals are you should schedule your training in a certain way. There are many different protocols or "plans" that are available on the market many of them are not science based so be careful with what you spend your money on. A diet plan is the hardest to make so if you find yourself truly in need of one you should get it custom made. A training plan is relativly easy to make with some research and understanding of splits and recovery window for muscles.
